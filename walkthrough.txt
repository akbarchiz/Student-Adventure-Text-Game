Walkthrough:
1. Go to the university and speak with Ivan. He hands you the weapon.
2. Go to the basketball court and murder Bryan.
3. Now that you have money, go to "Labofarm" and buy chemicals.
4. Go to the lab and make some fuel.
5. Proceed to the Launch area to get your prize.

Features:
1. It is fine if the player murders Oliver. As stated in the narrative, the rocket design is complete and all that is required for launch is fuel.
2. Because it is an open world, the player has the ability to kill anyone.
3. If you kill the seller in Labofarm, you will be arrested immediately.
4. Your desired level rises by one for each kill.
5. If your desired level is 5, you are arrested.
